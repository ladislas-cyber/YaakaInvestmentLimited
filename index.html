Our Projects

E-Waste Recycling Plant

Our state-of-the-art E-Waste Recycling Plant has the capacity to process 1 ton of e-waste per hour, managing an impressive 50,000 tons annually. This initiative significantly reduces CO2 emissions and prevents harmful open-air burning, contributing to a cleaner environment.

Yaaka Solar Systems

We provide innovative solar solutions designed to bring electricity to rural areas across Zambia. Our systems enhance access to power for essential needs like mobile device charging, bridging the energy gap and empowering communities.

Green Construction

In partnership with CIB Engineering Services Limited, we implement green construction practices utilizing our proprietary Yaaka carbon capture and incinerator system technology. This collaboration effectively reduces carbon emissions generated from the waste management sector, promoting sustainable urban development.
